Description

Experience the heart of Zurich in this beautifully appointed suite in the historic Old Town. The Bellevue Suite offers a perfect blend of classic Swiss charm and modern comfort, featuring high ceilings, original hardwood floors, and large windows overlooking cobblestone streets.


This spacious one-bedroom apartment is ideal for business travelers and couples seeking a premium stay in Zurich's most prestigious neighborhood. Walking distance to Bahnhofstrasse, Lake Zurich, and major corporate offices.


The apartment includes a fully equipped kitchen, high-speed internet, and weekly housekeeping service.
